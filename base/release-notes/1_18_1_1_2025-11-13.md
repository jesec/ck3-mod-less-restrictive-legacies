# Update 1.18.1.1 Changelog

## Bugfixes

-   Fixed a crash that may happen when opening the Royal Court in 1178.
-   Fixed a crash that could happen when opening the Situations window.
-   Southeast Asian commoners now know about belts, allowing them to keep their pants on.
-   Fixed the lvl 2 and 3 Determination Aspect having the wrong parameter set, preventing Watch-houses from being built if you leveled up your aspect.
-   Fixed lvl 4+ Paddy Fields having the wrong upgrade requirements.
-   Han now starts with innovation\_arched\_saddle in 867 so they can recruit Heavy Cavalry (which they historically had access to).
-   Reduced the strength of Shenbigong crossbowmen somewhat.
-   Reverted some changes to Men-at-Arms stats.
-   Removed a faulty treasury modifier from the Heirloom Seal and replaced it with domain tax and a capital development bonus.
-   The Japanese throne room will no longer appear outside Japan/for non-Japanese cultures.
-   Great Liao now starts with a large army, making them able to rival Song in 1066.
-   Shatuo culture now has the Iron Cavalry tradition.
